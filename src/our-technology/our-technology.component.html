Our Technology

Our innovation, GlaucoSolve Glasses is a non-invasive, wearable, cost-effective, and remote monitoring solution that uses ultrasound transducers, receivers, and Arduino modules to monitor IOP.

By applying acoustic force to the cornea and analyzing the reflected signal, we can compute various ultrasound parameters and correlate them to get the patient's eye pressure.

Realtime data will be sent to a mobile app allowing physicians to monitor long-term glaucoma progression remotely and alerting patients on when they should apply eye drops, mitigating optic nerve damage.

Moreover, we plan to develop a machine learning model with collected data, offering AI insights into glaucoma diagnosis.

By learning nuanced IOP patterns in patients with all stages of glaucoma, our model will enable ophthalmologists to spot signs of glaucoma and predict patients' future IOP trajectory to evaluate the effectiveness of treatments just days after prescription.
